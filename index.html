Emotionally Focused Counseling

At Emotionally Focused Counseling, our highly trained and experienced therapists provide confidential, convenient and effective psychotherapy services to teens and adults.

We help you find your way onto a healthier path.

We specialize in managing complex change. We know that all of life’s transitions-- from childhood through emancipation; career, marriage, birth of children, divorce, empty nest, death of a loved one, loss and retirement— can thrown you into a tailspin.

With our help you will:

  • Get relief from the symptoms that are causing distress;
  • Identify the core problems that prevent you from feeling happy and well;
  • Become expert at understanding and expressing your own emotional needs and desires;
  • Get the tools necessary for living the life you’ve always wanted.


Hours


Intake Sessions

Intake sessions are scheduled between 9:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. Monday through Friday.

After-hour intake sessions are available for an additional fee.


Existing Clients

Established clients may schedule between 9:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. and will have access to support services via text, phone, or on-site support during business hours.

After-hour sessions and support services are available for an additional fee.
