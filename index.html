An unexamined life is like an unopened envelope…

At Emotionally Focused Counseling, our highly trained and experienced therapists provide confidential, convenient and effective psychotherapy services to adolescents, adults and couples who are wanting to get relief from emotional distress and wanting to make meaningful changes in their lives.

We specialize in managing complex change. And we recognize that all of life’s transitions—from childhood through emancipation, career, marriage, birth of children, divorce, empty nest, death of a loved one, loss and retirement—can throw you into a tailspin.

We can help you learn how it is our need for emotional bonding— like that between mother and child —that guides our choices during all of these landmarks and in all love relationships throughout our lives.

With our help you will:

  • Get relief from symptoms that are causing distress;
  • Identify the core problems that prevent you from feeling happy and well;
  • Become expert at understanding and expressing your own emotional needs and desires.

We invite you to let us help you find your way onto a healthy life path and learn how to live the life you’ve always wanted.
