Meet Our Counselors

Kathleen Shields, Ph.D.

Kathleen Shields is a Licensed Clinical Psychologist in Louisville, Colorado. Dr. Shields was awarded her doctorate in psychology from the University of Denver and undergraduate and graduate degrees from the University of Colorado and the University of Pittsburgh. Dr. Shields provides comprehensive psychotherapy services to individuals (adolescents and adults), couples and families. She has been in private practice in Boulder and Louisville, Colorado for more than 25 years following over twenty years as a school psychologist. Dr. Shields has a special interest in the neuroscience of behavior and served as a consulting psychologist for a Denver-based brain imaging company, a clinic that specialized in the research and understanding of the brain-behavior connection.

Nancy Hamilton, L.C.S.W.

Nancy Hamilton is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker in Louisville, Colorado. She was awarded her Bachelor of Arts in Humanities and Psychology from The University of Colorado, Boulder and her Master of Social Work from Smith College, Northampton, Massachusetts. Nancy provides comprehensive therapy and executive coaching services to individuals, couples and families. Nancy offers in-office sessions as well as phone, Skype and Facetime sessions for out-of-state couples or couples that prefer to receive services from the comfort of their home or office.
